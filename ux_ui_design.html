Greetings!

My name is Ethan Smith. I am a Computer Graphics Technology Graduate from the Purdue School of Science and Engineering with a concentration on Interactive Multimedia Design. I am primarily interested in UX / UI Design as I am constantly analyzing big and small brand name user interfaces, and am always thinking of better ways to design user interfaces. I like to ask people questions about their experience on their devices for commonly used apps, pointing out flaws in the design that they would normally not consciously notice, and offer design-solutions.
